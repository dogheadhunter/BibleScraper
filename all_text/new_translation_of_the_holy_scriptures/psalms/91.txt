+ 10 No calamity will befall you, + And not even a plague will draw near to your tent. + 11 For he will give his own angels a command concerning you, + To guard you in all your ways. + 12 Upon their hands they will carry you, + That you may not strike your foot against any stone. + 13 Upon the young lion and the cobra you will tread;
