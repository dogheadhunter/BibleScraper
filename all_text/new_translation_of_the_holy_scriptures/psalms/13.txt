A melody of David. 13 How long, O Jehovah, will you forget me? + Forever? + How long will you conceal your face from me? + 2 How long shall I set resistance in my soul, Grief in my heart by day? How long will my enemy be exalted over me? + 3 Do look [upon me]; answer me, O Jehovah my God. Do make my eyes shine, + that I may not fall asleep in death, + 4 That my enemy may not say: “I have won out over him!” [That] my adversaries themselves may [not] be joyful because I am made to stagger. + 5 As for me, in your loving-kindness * I have trusted; + Let my heart be joyful in your salvation. + 6 I will sing to Jehovah, for he has dealt rewardingly with me. * + Footnotes ^ Or, “loyal love.” ^ LXXVg add: “And I shall make melody to the name of Jehovah the Most High.” See 7:17.
