All these were the sons of Aʹzel. 39 And the sons of Eʹshek his brother were Uʹlam his firstborn, Jeʹush the second and E·liphʹe·let the third. 40 And the sons of Uʹlam came to be valiant, + mighty men, bending the bow, + and having many sons + and grandsons, a hundred and fifty. All these were from the sons of Benjamin.
