Psalm 128:1-6 A Song of the Ascents. 128 Happy is everyone who fears Jehovah, + Who walks in His ways. + 2 You will eat what your hands worked hard to produce. You will be happy and will enjoy prosperity. + 3 Your wife will be like a fruitful vine within your house; + Your sons will be like shoots of an olive tree around your table. 4 Look! That is how the man who fears Jehovah Will be blessed. + 5 Jehovah will bless you from Zion. May you see Jerusalem prosper all the days of your life + 6 And see the sons of your sons.
